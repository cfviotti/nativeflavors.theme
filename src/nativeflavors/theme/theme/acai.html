Grateful Acaí To eat with respect

A small, purple fruit. A great gift from nature to the man kind. Cultivated in the Amazon region, it is rich in antioxidants and fibers, and has high levels of vitamin C.
The word Acai comes from the local indigenous language, Tupi-Guarani, and means "fruit that cries". An allusion to the delicious juice inside the fruit. Crying with joy.
